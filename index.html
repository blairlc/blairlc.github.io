I am an Interactive Producer at Quatrefoil Associates, an exhibition design firm. I bring stories and concepts to life with interactive technology. You can see a selection of exhibits I've worked on in my portfolio.

I graduated from University of Maryland, College Park in December 2018 with a B.S. in Information Science, which is the study of the relationship between people, information, and technology. Some of my favorite classes included User-Centered Design, Art and the Museum World, and Coding for Live Performance.

While in college, I was the undergrad representative on the President's Commission on Disability Issues. I spoke at eight disability-related events and organized several others. I continue to push for accessibility through my professional work.
