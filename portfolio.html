Carrier

Carrier is a platformer featuring a carrier pigeon who isn't very good at flying, so he has to collect coins to take the bus home. The game was created in under 48 hours during Global Game Jam 2018. I did the programming using Unity 2D. The art was created by Nat DeMenthon.
